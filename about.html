About BSfitness

BSfitness is your trusted companion on the journey to health and wellness. We provide fitness plans tailored for all age groups—whether you're aiming to build muscle, grow taller, or simply feel better through yoga.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help everyone lead a healthier lifestyle by providing accessible, easy-to-follow exercise routines and nutritional guidance.

Who We Serve

  • Children and teens seeking height growth guidance
  • Adults aiming to build muscle or stay fit
  • Anyone interested in yoga and general wellness

Why We Started

As a couple passionate about fitness and coding, we built BSfitness to share our knowledge and motivate others to take care of their health using simple, web-based tools.

Meet the Team

Created by Binaya and his wife Siwani— learners, coders, and fitness enthusiasts on a mission to make fitness fun and accessible.
